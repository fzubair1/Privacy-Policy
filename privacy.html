Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Renter's Mate. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name and email address (optional)
  • Property Information: Property addresses, rental details, and tenancy information
  • Documents: Documents and photos you upload to the app
  • Communication Logs: Records of your communications with landlords and agents

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Renter's Mate service
  • Store your rental property information locally on your device
  • Process your subscription payments through Apple's App Store
  • Send you important service updates and notifications

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal information, property details, documents, or any other app data on our servers. Your information remains on your device and is backed up through your iCloud account if you have iCloud backup enabled.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription payments and app distribution
  • iCloud: For optional data backup (controlled by your device settings)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. Since data is stored locally on your device, you control access through your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app)
  • Export your data

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
